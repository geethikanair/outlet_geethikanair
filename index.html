Welcome to my website!

Hi, I'm Geethika, a high school sophomore (Class of 2027) passionate about computer engineering, computer graphics design, and community involvement. I've taken AP Computer Science Principles and computer graphics 1 & 2, where I developed a love for character design and visual storytelling. Currently, I serve as the Social Media Officer at EmpowerHer Education, Group Lead for Website Development at Hope Avenue, Secretary at GIFT, and Website Developer at Youth4Youth. I'm also Vice President of the MV Book Club, and involved in the Library Advisory Committee and Contra Costa County Library's Teen Advisory Board. Outside of school, I enjoy tennis and bharatanatyam, and I'm passionate about learning, teamwork, and creating inclusive solutions.
