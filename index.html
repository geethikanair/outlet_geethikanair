Welcome to my website!

Hi, I’m Geethika, a high school sophomore (Class of 2027) with a strong interest in computer engineering, computer graphics design, and helping out the community. I’ve taken AP Computer Science Principles and computer graphics 1 and 2, where I discovered a love for character design, visual storytelling, and user-focused digital creation. I am currently the Social Media Officer at EmpowerHer Education, Group Lead for Website Development at Hope Avenue, Secretary and Intern at GIFT, and Website Developer at Youth4Youth.

In addition to my work in tech and advocacy, I’m actively involved in my school and local library communities as Vice President of the MV Book Club, a member of the Library Advisory Committee, and a Teen Advisory Board Member for the Contra Costa County Library.

Outside of academics and extracurriculars, I enjoy playing tennis and bharatanatyam. But mainly, I’m passionate about learning, collaborating across diverse teams, and creating inclusive, purpose-driven solutions.
